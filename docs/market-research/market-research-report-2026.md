## Executive Summary

Line Optimizer enters a growing market for manufacturing capacity planning software at a favorable time. The convergence of supply chain disruptions, nearshoring trends, and Industry 4.0 adoption has created urgent demand for accessible capacity planning tools. However, the market remains bifurcated: enterprise solutions (SAP, Siemens, Kinaxis) serve large manufacturers with six-figure budgets, while mid-market plants rely on error-prone Excel spreadsheets.
