## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| No warm contacts respond | Expand to 2nd-degree connections, industry events |
| Pilots don't convert | Review success criteria, improve onboarding |
| Product gaps discovered | Prioritize quick fixes, document for roadmap |
| Pricing resistance | Test value messaging, consider pilot-only discount |
| Time constraints (product vs sales) | Block dedicated selling time, protect it |
